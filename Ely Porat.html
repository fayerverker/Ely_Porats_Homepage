Short Bio

Ely Porat has completed his Ph.D. at the age of 20 under the supervision of Prof. Amihood Amir at the year 2000, at the same year he was appointed as a faculty member (assistant professor) in the CS faculty of Bar Ilan University. Porat has became associate professor in the department at the year of 2007 and he is full professor since 2012.

He has been a visiting professor at Universiry of California, Berkeley, the University of Michigan, Tel-Aviv University and in Google corporation (both in Mountain View, California and in Tel Aviv, Israel)

During a long and significant military service, Ely commanded a strategic research team within an elite cyber unit of the Israel Defense Force and led a project that won the prestigious Israeli Defense Award. He also one of thr initiators and founders of of Arazim academic mathematical-technologic officers training program for recruits with outstanding academic abilities in the sciences and leadership potential. Since 2008 Ely chirs the Mathematical insitiute ot the israeli MOD.

Students Recruitment

Professor Ely Porat is recruiting postdoctoral researchers and Ph.D. Students.

For postdoctoral researchers, We are looking for researchers in a mature stage of their academic capabilities, with a Ph.D. degree in Computer Science or Mathematics and with a strong publication record in Theoretical Computer Science. Our threshold for applicants is at least three papers accepted to top tier conferences (e.g. STOC, FOCS or SODA).

For Ph.D. students we seek students with M.Sc. Degree in Computer Science or related field which has at least one publication in an international peer reviewed conference or journal.

Researchers and graduate students that will be accepted to the group may pursue their own line of interest but a focus related to the existing research areas of the group is very encouraged.

If you see yourself as fit to these conditions, please send your application (including a cover letter, a curriculum vitae, a list of publications, and the names and contact information of at least two references) to recruit.postdoc@datalab.cs.biu.ac.il. Please attach relevant documents as pdf files.
